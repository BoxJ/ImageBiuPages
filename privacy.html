2. How we use cookies and similar technologies

When you visit our client, web pages or use our services, we may use cookies or similar technologies. Cookies are data files stored by the server on your device that can store device preferences and settings, and record which parts of the site the device has visited. Cookies can only be read by the server that created the cookie. We use cookies and other similar technologies to provide you with more convenient, safer, and more in line with your preferences. We identify your authentication status when you visit, count visits, and realize your points accumulation and other functions. You can decide whether to block cookies. You need to check your browser settings or consult the browser vendor for specific operations. However, blocking cookies may affect your access to and use of our services. For example, you may need to log in again, and you may not be able to accurately obtain points, badges, user level upgrades and other services.
